# Research Disclaimer & Contact Policy

This repository contains educational research, technical notes, and architectural case studies regarding software design, client-side state handling, and network protocols.

## Research Ethics

1. **Non-Disruptive Intent:** None of the material published here is intended to bypass access controls, disrupt services, or harm third-party systems.
2. **Abstracted Samples:** Code examples are simplified, refactored, or anonymized to demonstrate general software patterns rather than functional exploits against specific targets.
3. **Public Assets Only:** All analyses are conducted using publicly accessible assets and network traffic provided directly to standard browsers or client applications.
